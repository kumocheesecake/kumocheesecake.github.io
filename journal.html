"Raspberry x Original Flavor"

The all-time best-selling original flavor, combined with Raspberry, managed to create a wonderful flavor combination.

Various Aroma, Tastes and Textures

We added different kinds of citrus, which pair perfectly with the sourness of Raspberry. The sweet aroma of Vanilla adds a great balance to the overall flavor. If you like some textures on your cheesecake, please be sure to add our "Charred Crunchy Butter Sauce" and "Cacao Crumble". Butter sauce will add a slightly salty taste, with the addition of crunchy texture. You'll love it if you love the combination of sweet and salty.

Recommended Toppings: Charred Crunchy Butter Sauce & Cacao Crumble

Cheesecake Biscoff Sandwich

You could really get creative with our cheesecake! This time, we suggest you to go out and buy some Biscoff Lotus biscuits and sandwich the cheesecake in between them. Try it and thank us later.

The slightly smokey flavor coming out of Biscoff pairs perfectly well with our light and rich, yet refreshing cheesecake.

Recommended Temperature: Out-Of-The-Fridge
